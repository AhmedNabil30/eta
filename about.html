Our Story

Pioneering Renewable Energy in Egypt

ETEAS Energy Technology and Electrical Automation Solution is a partnership company established according to Egyptian law No. 95 for 1992, with commercial registration No. 14927. We have built our reputation through extensive experience in low voltage protection switchgears manufacturing and heavy project management with well-studied time schedules and cost control.

Our staff are fully aware of international codes and standards, prioritizing safety with high technical personal skills. We coordinate effectively between various projects, providing optimal solutions that meet client requirements while maintaining strict time schedules and providing periodic progress reports.
